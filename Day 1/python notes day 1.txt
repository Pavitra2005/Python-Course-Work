Token:
------
the smallest unit of a program is called token

ex:
----
n=int(input())
if(n%2==0):
  print("even")
else:
  print("odd")

tokens are divided into 5 types:
1.identifiers : var,function name,class name,module name,package name
2.literal : int,float,double,str,boolean,list
3.keywords: if,else,for,from
4.operations: +,-,*,%,<,<=,==
5.special characters:(),'
